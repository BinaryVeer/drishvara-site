# AG75C — Expanded Star Reflection Output Bank

## Decision

AG75C creates an internal 39-record Star Reflection output bank at
governed baseline `b1f5b8534446d2acc0caf70c4ed07fa296814f1a`. The reviewed candidate plan
SHA-256 is `29386a5e0ff312e1a4074669ba536dd1d89982af34b0c4dce3b92ab6c009962e`.

The bank contains:

- 27 exact-window records, one for each governed Nakshatra index;
- 12 contextual, limitation, unavailable or ambiguity records; and
- complete coverage of all 13 AG75B resolution states.

## Exact-window records

An exact-window record may be selected only after AG75B proves that
the complete entered `HH:MM` minute lies inside one governed
Nakshatra interval. Each record retains the governed index/name pair
and uses the Nakshatra name only as a symbolic reflection label.

The records do not assert fixed traits, predict events or provide
professional advice.

## Non-exact records

Every other AG75B state has one distinct safe record. These records
contain no Nakshatra identity and never claim an exact birth star.

## Output structure

Every record includes the five governed sections: Reflection Basis,
Reflective Theme, Question for Reflection, Grounding Practice and
Pilot Limitation.

Runtime metadata entries are schema slots only. The bank embeds no
live date, place, timezone, coordinate or personal value.

## Safety and non-mutation boundary

AG75C performed no live runtime call and made no public UI, public
JavaScript, database, Edge Function, deployment or runtime-flag
change. It embeds no name, date of birth, birth time, raw location or
coordinates.

No unresolved state is converted into a fabricated Panchang value,
Nakshatra identity or deterministic prediction.

## AG75D handoff

AG75D may wire the immutable AG75C bank to the controlled public Star
Reflection UI and the existing closed SUP02 Panchang function. It
must keep all inputs session-only, use no browser secret, preserve
every AG75B failure and ambiguity state, and leave the database,
Edge Function, runtime release and output bank unchanged.
