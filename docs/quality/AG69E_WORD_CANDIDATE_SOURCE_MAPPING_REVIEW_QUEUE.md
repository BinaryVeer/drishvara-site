# AG69E — Word Candidate Source Mapping and Review Queue

AG69E maps candidate Word of the Day records to candidate source families and creates review queues.

## Created

- Candidate source mapping.
- Source review queue.
- Linguistic review queue.
- Internal textual-discipline review queue.
- Source-reference coverage check.
- No-content-ingestion and no-approval audit.

## Important

Mapping is not approval. It does not confirm word meaning, Sanskrit form, transliteration, etymology or classical usage.

## Word selection context doctrine

AG69E also records that future Word of the Day selection must not be arbitrary or AI-generated.

Once an approved word bank and approved daily-context bank exist, selection should consider:

- Tithi.
- Vara / weekday.
- Festival or observance context.
- Seasonal, historical or traditional relevance.
- Source-backed symbolic or planetary association where applicable.
- Previously used word duplication check.

If no valid context match is available, the selector may fall back only to an approved Vedic/Indic value-word from the approved bank.

This doctrine is not active in AG69E. No Panchang value is generated, no tithi/vara selection is activated, and generated/word-of-day.json remains unchanged.
